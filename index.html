Bangkok, Thailand

Bangkok, the capital of Thailand, is known for its vibrant street life, ornate shrines, and bustling markets. Visitors can explore the Grand Palace, Wat Arun, and enjoy Thai cuisine in its numerous street food stalls.

Dubai, UAE

Dubai is a city of superlatives, famous for its ultramodern architecture, luxury shopping, and lively nightlife scene. Key attractions include the Burj Khalifa, Palm Jumeirah, and Dubai Mall.

London, UK

London, the capital of England, is a cultural melting pot known for its historic landmarks such as the Buckingham Palace, Tower Bridge, and British Museum. It offers a blend of history, art, fashion, and cuisine.

New York City, USA

New York City, often called the "Big Apple," is a global hub of finance, culture, and entertainment. Visitors can explore iconic landmarks like Times Square, Central Park, Statue of Liberty, and enjoy Broadway shows.
